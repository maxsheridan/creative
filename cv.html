Apart from founding and directing the center, I also taught short story writing, live storytelling, and creative blogging, and managed its digital presence.

2005-Present

Creative Writer

When I’m not writing copy, I write fiction and journalism. I’ve published over 40 short stories and articles, and two novels — Dillo (Down and Out Books, 2018) and God’s Speedboat (Outcast Press, 2023).

Creative Direction
